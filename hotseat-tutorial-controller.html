Annotated Reference Guide
1
Header — Session Status
Shows live guest count, session timer, and floor live indicator. Guest count updates as guests scan QR codes on the floor.
2
Live Audio Bar
Paste your AzuraCast stream URL and hit Go On Air to broadcast your voice live. Green dot means you're live. Guests hear you in real time.
→ Hit Test first to verify your stream before going on air.
3
Section Selector
Choose which section receives your broadcast. All Sections reaches every connected guest. Target a specific section for floor-specific alerts.
4
Hot Seat Manager
Set the prize amount, start the countdown, and HotSeat fires the drawing automatically at zero — including populating the message and broadcasting it.
→ Fire Now skips the timer and fires immediately.
5
Alert Type
Select the alert category before broadcasting. The type appears in the guest's alert feed and color-codes the broadcast log. Hot Seat fires in red, Jackpot in green.
6
Message + Toggles
Type your broadcast message or select Quick Fire. Enable Ticker to scroll the message across guest screens. Enable Live Audio to send your stream URL with the alert.
7
Quick Fire Buttons
Pre-written messages for the most common alerts. Tap any button to populate the message field instantly — then edit if needed before firing.
8
Fire Broadcast
Sends the message to all connected guests in the selected section simultaneously. Confirmation appears below the button. Entry is logged automatically.
→ Message field clears automatically after firing.
