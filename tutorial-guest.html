What Each Element Does
1
Scrolling Ticker
Appears across the top of the screen when you enable Ticker on a broadcast. Scrolls the message continuously. Hot seat winners, jackpot amounts, and tournament standings work especially well here.
2
Section Chips
Shows the guest which section they're connected to based on the QR code they scanned. When you target a specific section in the controller, only guests in that section receive the alert.
3
Live Audio Player
Appears automatically when you fire a broadcast with Live Audio enabled. Guest taps Listen Live to hear your voice via AzuraCast stream. Animated waveform shows live signal.
→ Only appears when you're on air and include audio in a broadcast.
4
Hot Seat Countdown
Live countdown synced to the Hot Seat Manager on your controller. Guests watch the clock count down in real time — building anticipation before every drawing. Prize amount shown prominently.
5
Alert Banner
Full-screen banner that drops from the top of the phone when you fire a broadcast. Hot seat banners appear in red, general alerts in gold. Auto-dismisses after 14 seconds or guest taps Dismiss.
→ This is the high-attention moment — guests won't miss it.
6
Alerts Feed
Running history of every broadcast you've fired this session. New alerts appear at the top. Color-coded by type — red for Hot Seat, green for Jackpot, gold for general. Guests can scroll back to review.
7
Caesars Rewards Strip
Persistent branding element at the bottom of the screen. Reinforces the Caesars Rewards program and keeps brand presence on screen between alerts.
